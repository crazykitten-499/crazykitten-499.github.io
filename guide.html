About Animation

This section is about the process of animation

Into Animation

Digital Animations are quite easy to make, you only need a decent PC and some time.

The first step is to make the object that will move.

Next I add a scene

Then I add movement, or in this case, simulation mechanics

I hide unnecesary objects

I add lighting and more background

Add color

I angle the camera or animate it

I then bake and render the animation to complete it
